Welcome to AuthorBeacon

Whether you’re just starting your writing journey or you’ve already published books, AuthorBeacon is here to help you shine. This platform was created by an indie writer, for indie writers.

Our mission is simple: to provide a safe, welcoming, and affordable space where independent authors can share their work, grow their skills, and connect with a supportive community.

I built AuthorBeacon because I believe in your voice. Your story matters. Let’s build something great together.

— Jeremy Nagy, Founder of AuthorBeacon

Our Mission

To empower, support, and celebrate independent authors at every step of their journey.

Our Services

  • Writing guides and helpful resources
  • Book cover design and formatting assistance
  • Support for self-publishing and indie-friendly options
  • Opportunities for author collaboration and networking
